Mangrove

Mangroves are important to people because they help stabilize Florida's coastline ecosystem and prevent erosion.
Mangroves provide natural infrastructure and protection to nearby populated areas by preventing
erosion and absorbing storm surge impacts during extreme weather events such as hurricanes.
Mangrove plant products are used as tannins, honey, medicinal products,and thatch
